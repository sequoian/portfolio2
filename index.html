About

Hello, my name is Zachary Askins and I am a web developer currently living in Anaheim, California. I have been programming as a hobby for many years, and I am now ready to turn my passion into a career! I see the web as an exciting medium that is still growing and maturing, and I want to be a part of that future. Having spent roughly half my life on the Internet, I have aquired a design philosophy that is hopefully reflected in my work: apps should be simple enough to be approachable to new users, yet also empower those who use it day after day.

So what can I actually do? I'm glad you asked. I can build elegant and responsive web pages and apps. I am fluent in JavaScript and familiar with the cutting-edge features of ES2015 and beyond. I have expertise in React and use Redux to manage my data. As for the backend, I use Node.js with Express, store my data in SQL, and create my APIs using RESTful design.
